How this is calculated

Severity-weighted score
Fatal×5 + Grievous×3 + Minor (hospitalized)×2 + Minor (non-hosp.)×1 + Non-injury×0.5, summed per district.
Avg. road-km to nearest hospital
Mean, across the district's accidents, of each accident's nearest hospital by actual OSRM road distance (not straight-line).
Accidents per hospital
accident count ÷ hospital count in that district.
Unreachable count
Accidents whose nearest hospital by road is more than 50 km away, or has no candidate hospital at all.
